ABOUT US

In the present work, we have proposed HarvestHub: AI-Driven Agricultural Insights, a web-based platform aimed at supporting farmers in making more informed decisions to improve crop productivity and reduce losses. Agriculture faces numerous challenges such as unpredictable weather, pest outbreaks, and suboptimal farming practices, leading to decreased crop yields and financial difficulties for farmers.

The proposed platform will three key features : personalized crop recommendations, accurate yield predictions, and early plant disease detection. These features, powered by advanced AI models, will help farmers select the best crops for their environment, predict the potential yield, and identify diseases early, enabling timely interventions.

By providing farmers with these critical insights, HarvestHub aims to improve agricultural practices, boost productivity, and enhance the overall efficiency of the farming process, ultimately contributing to more sustainable and profitable farming.
